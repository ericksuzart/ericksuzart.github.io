Detail

Held by Empresa Júnior de Computação da UFBA (infoJr) and aimed at the entire regional Information and Communication Technology (ICT) community, which includes students from public and private universities, as well as professors, researchers and professionals in the area, SEMCOMP aims to inclusion, motivation and transformation of the technological market and society by promoting discussions that allow the community to move, in addition to fostering technological entrepreneurship in the State of Bahia.

There were more than 30 hours of activities during the 4 days of the event, with the participation of professionals and researchers, as well as names from the national and regional IT market. Several practical activities were part of the event's program, in addition to mini-courses and workshops.
